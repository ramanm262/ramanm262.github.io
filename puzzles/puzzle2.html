Puzzle 2

Imagine you are in an ancient tomb. You come across a massive stone statue depicting some personality of a past age. To your surprise, a voice emanates from within the statue, saying to you:

Answer my riddle correctly, and I shall reveal to you the location of long-lost treasure.

I can support nearly anything,
but I can lift nothing.
My form can endure nearly anything,
but on my own, I can recover from nothing.
I could look like nearly anyone,
but I would likely fool no one.
I do not lie, and I do not lie. What am I?

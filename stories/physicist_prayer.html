The Physicist's Prayer

God, please tell me
(if I am worth your time;
if my desperate cries for truth move you)
why electrons act the way they do?

If I had flown
ninety million miles,
with the power of the Sun to take me there,
I wouldn't let myself be stopped by air.

I wouldn't want
to have no say in where
I were to end my life eventually
just because that spot was great potentially.

Relax, my son.
Electrons do not think.
They follow natural procedure.
Electrons are not conscious things.
Mind you, you aren't either.

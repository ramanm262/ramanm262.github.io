Looking Back

Your high horse has a broken leg. You know what must be done.
How much grimier the street you grew up on seems
from your glassy gilded towers' heights.
It's got to hurt to crane your neck like that.
Now that you've forced the important aspects into past tense,
any time you speak, I remember:
 "Advice from a butterfly is as good as advice from a goldfish."
